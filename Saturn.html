Saturn

This is Saturn.

Saturn is the sixth planet away from the sun. Saturn's rings are brighter than Saturn is itself. 1 year on Saturn is 29.5 years on Earth. Saturn, like Jupiter, is a gas giant. If there was a pool bigenough, Saturn could float in the water. Let's hope it doesn't lose any rings! Saturn has 17 known moons. One of Saturns moons is Titan. Titan has huge lakes of crude oil. Titan also has seasonal changes and light rain. The atmosphere on Titan is nearly the same as Earth's atmosphere was 4 billion years ago. Saturn is beautiful, with a moon that may be populated with humans in the future.
