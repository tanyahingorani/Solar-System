Neptune

This is Neptune.

Neptune is the farthest planet from the sun. It is also the smallest gas giant. Neptune is extremely cold. The temperature at the top of the layer of clouds can be -350 degrees F, yet Neptune's core is hotter than the sun's surface. It takes 165 earth years for one year on Neptune. Neptune has 4 rings. Neptune also has 8 known moons. Neptune has winds that blow at about 1,200 miles per hour. Neptune was discovered by mathematical calculations. Neptune is a stormy planet!
