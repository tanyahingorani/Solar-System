Pluto

This is Pluto.

Pluto is the second largest and most famous dwarf planet. Pluto is reddish-colored. Pluto has one moon, called Charon. Because Charon is so big, Pluto and Charon are considered a double-planet link. Pluto also has 4 smaller moons- Styx, Nix, Kerberos, and Hydra. One day on Pluto is 6 days on Earth. One year on Pluto is 248 years on Earth. If I lived on Pluto, my birthday would come only once every 248 Earth years. I personally would not like that!
