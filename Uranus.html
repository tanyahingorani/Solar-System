Uranus

This is Uranus.

Uranus is the seventh planet from the sun. Uranus has 10 rings. Uranus has a 98 degree tilt to the side, and it's rings do too, so Uranus looks upside down. Uranus has 27 known moons. Uranus has a north pole that experiences 42 years of darkness, followed by 42 years of brightness. It is the same with the south pole. Uranus is also a gas giant. Uranus is 8 times the size of Earth.Uranus is big, but not as big as Jupiter. Uranus is one tipped over planet!
