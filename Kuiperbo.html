Kuiper belt

This is the Kuiper belt on the left and Haumea on the right.

The Kuiper belt is a hoop of asteroids and other space objects. It is beyond all the planets, even Neptune.

Haumea is one of the strangest objects in the solar system. It is a dwarf planet shaped like a football. When it is orbiting the sun, it goes end over end like a football in the field. Scientists call it the cosmic football. Haumea is like an M&M. The outside candy coating is the ice. The chocolate inside is the rock. It is located in the Kuiper belt. Haumea is a very bizarre dwarf planet.
