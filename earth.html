Earth

This is Earth.

Earth is our planet, the third planet from the sun. It is sometimes known as the blue planet because of all Earth's oceans. Earth is the only planet where all 3 forms of water (Liquid water, water vapor, and ice) exist. Sometimes planets that are similar to Earth are found, but slightly larger. These are called Super-earths. Super-earths can be found all over the universe. Our Earth is an amazing world with water and not many extreme temperatures. It is the perfect planet for life.
