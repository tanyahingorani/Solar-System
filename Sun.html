Sun

This is the Sun.

The sun is the only star you can see in the daytime. It is also the source of all light and heat. It is made up of 74% hydrogen and 25% helium. I wonder what the last 1% is?Do you know what sunspots are? They are spots on the sun that are more chilly than the rest of the sun. But they are still blazing hot! The sun has a core that is around 27 million degrees F. The sun has a surface that is around 10,00 degrees F. More than 1 million earths could fit inside the Sun. Can you believe how hot the sun is?

A kelvin is a unit used to measure temperature. Its abbreviation is K.
